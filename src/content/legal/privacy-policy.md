# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **omoka** (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website, join our waitlist, or use our upcoming services.

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect data necessary to provide our services, manage the waitlist, and deliver early-access benefits. This includes:

- **Required Information:**
  - **Email Address:** To secure your spot in the queue and contact you.
- **Optional Information (for Full Data Unlock):**
  To provide you with the "Free AI bug audit" and specialized rewards, we may collect:
  - **Professional Information:** Your primary role (e.g., Founder, QA Engineer) and team size.
  - **Company Information:** Your company website URL.
  - **Target Application Data:** The URL of the specific app or website where you want omoka to look for bugs.
- **Usage Data:**
  - IP address, device information, browser type, and behavioral metrics to improve our landing page performance.

## 2. Purpose of Data Use

We process your data for the following specific purposes:

- **Waitlist Management:** To notify you when we go live and provide your exclusive launch discount (Early Adopter Discount).
- **Service Delivery (AI Audit):** To analyze the "App or website URL" you provided and generate the promised "Free AI bug report" before launch.
- **Lead Segmentation:** To understand our audience (via Role and Team Size) and tailor our product development to better suit your needs.
- **Communication:** To send you updates about omoka’s progress and new features.
- **Security:** To prevent abuse and protect our infrastructure.

## 3. AI Data Handling

omoka is an AI-powered agent. We are committed to the highest privacy standards regarding AI processing:

- **Target Scanning:** If you provide a target URL for the "Free AI bug audit", you grant us permission to have our AI agent visit that public URL to perform visual and functional analysis.
- **No Training:** Your data, uploaded files, and app screenshots will **NEVER** be used to train our own or third-party AI models.
- **Enterprise Privacy:** We use Enterprise/API versions of providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google. Your data remains confidential within the session and is not used for model improvement by these providers.

## 4. Third-party Subprocessors

We partner with trusted services to host our site and analyze traffic:

- **Hosting & Infrastructure:** Data is stored on secure servers located in the European Union / US (depending on your primary server location).
- **Analytics:** We may use tools to understand user intent and improve conversion rates.
- **Email Services:** Used to manage our waitlist and send notifications.

## 5. Data Retention & Your Rights

- **Retention:** We keep your email and optional profile data for as long as you remain on our waitlist or become an active user.
- **Deletion:** You can request to be removed from the waitlist and have your data deleted at any time. Just email us at **support@omoka.ai**.
- **Access:** You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data.

## 6. Security

The security of your data is our top priority. We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS) to protect your information during transmission and storage.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as we transition from waitlist to full launch. Any significant changes will be notified via email.

If you have any questions, contact us at:
