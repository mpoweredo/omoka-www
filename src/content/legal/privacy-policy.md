# Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how omoka (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website, join our waitlist, or use our upcoming services.

## 1. Data We Collect

We only collect data necessary to provide our services and keep you updated:

* **Waitlist Information:** Email address provided when requesting early access.
* **Usage Data & Analytics:** IP address, device information, browser type, and behavioral metrics (via session recordings and heatmaps) to improve our landing page.
* **Communication:** Any information you provide when contacting us directly via email.

## 2. Purpose of Data Use

We process your data for the following purposes:

* **Waitlist Management:** To notify you when we go live and provide your exclusive launch discount.
* **Product Updates:** To send you information about omoka’s progress and new features.
* **Service Improvement:** To analyze how visitors interact with our site to improve user experience.
* **Security:** To prevent abuse and protect our infrastructure.

## 3. AI Data Handling (Upcoming Service)

Omoka is an AI-powered agent. We are committed to the highest privacy standards regarding AI processing:

* **No Training:** Your data, uploaded files, and app screenshots will **NEVER** be used to train our own or third-party AI models.
* **Enterprise Privacy:** we use Enterprise/API versions of providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google. Your data remains confidential within the session.

## 4. Third-party Subprocessors

We partner with trusted services to host our site and analyze traffic:

* **Hosting:** Data is stored on secure servers located in the European Union.
* **Analytics:** We may use tools like Google Analytics or Microsoft Clarity to understand user intent.
* **Email Services:** Used to manage our waitlist and send notifications.

## 5. Data Retention & Your Rights

* **Retention:** We keep your email for as long as you remain on our waitlist. 
* **Deletion:** You can request to be removed from the waitlist at any time. Just email us at support@omoka.dev.
* **Access:** You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data.

## 6. Security

The security of your data is our top priority. We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS) to protect your information during transmission.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as we transition from waitlist to full launch. Any significant changes will be notified via email.

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at:
**Email:** support@omoka.dev
